Welcome to Basketball

Basketball is one of the most popular and fast-paced sports in the world. Played by millions, it has inspired countless athletes and fans. Whether you play in a gym or watch the NBA, basketball unites people of all ages and backgrounds!

Fun Facts About Basketball

  • Basketball was invented in 1891 by Dr. James Naismith.
  • The first game of basketball was played with a soccer ball and two peach baskets.
  • The NBA was founded in 1946 and is now the premier basketball league in the world.
  • Michael Jordan, considered one of the greatest players, won 6 NBA championships with the Chicago Bulls.
  • Basketball became an Olympic sport in 1936 at the Berlin Games.
  • Basketball has reached it's peak in the past decade, as the game has grown more advanced and exciting.
